WELCOME...

Welcome to the Delta State Library website! We're excited to offer you a modern and user-friendly platform to access all our resources and services anytime, anywhere. Whether you're a lifelong learner, a student seeking academic support, or simply looking for a quiet space to explore your interests, we have something for you.

what we do

Membership Registration

Unlock a world of library privileges by becoming a member of Delta State Library. Our easy and efficient membership registration process allows you to access personalized services, manage your borrowing history, and stay updated on the latest library offerings. Join us today and be a part of a community that values knowledge and connectivity.
